Lahore's urban expansion

Lahore has been expanding its urban boundaries exponentially as shown below.

The Urban boundary of Lahore in 2013 was 37,142 hectares, increasing at an average annual rate of 3.6% since 2000 and 3% since 1991, moving from a south eastern spread towards a circular outward growth today.
